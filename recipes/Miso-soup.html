Miso soup

back to index

Description

This miso soup is full of savory flavors. Add more dashi to your soup if you want a stronger stock. You can use yellow, white, or red miso paste for this soup â€” yellow miso is sweet and creamy, red miso is stronger and saltier.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons dashi granules
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package silken tofu, diced
  • 2 green onions, sliced diagonally into 1/2 inch pieces

Directions

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions.
  4. Simmer and serve.
